Who We Are

We are a clinical therapy practice offering services to Individuals, Couples, Families and Children.
Our mission at TGC is to provide counseling services for as many people as possible, regardless of socioeconomic status.

For clients who need services but lack insurance coverage, we offer sliding scale counseling through our foundation, which is a 501c(3). Therapists at Transformative Growth offer counseling for Couples, Families, Children and Individuals

Getting Started

Our therapy options are vast. We believe in offering therapy in a setting that makes you comfortable, which can be traditional or non-traditional. Whatever your preference, we believe your comfort in the setting helps encourage a dynamic where you and your therapist are in a space that fits your needs.

Payment & Insurance

Payment & Insurance The standard professional fee is $150 per session. We accept self-pay and insurance, including out of network plans. Insurance usually covers the professional fee, minus any deductibles or co-pays. Most plans have out of network benefits that can be accessed, giving you more freedom to choose where you go.


As a part of our mission to help our local communities, we offer sliding scale services for people and families who are uninsured or underinsured. Please inquire with Jessica.

  • We are in network with
  • BCBS
  • Cigna
  • Compsych
  • United Behavioral Health
  • For Children only:
    • Medicaid BCBS Family
  • Community insurance plan

    • You can also use your Flex card or HSA, cash, check or debit/credit card. Payment is due at the time services are delivered. We bill and send documents through an electronic health platform – you’ll be sent instructions for registering with our practice.
